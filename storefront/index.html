About Serendib

A gem house that keeps its own record

Serendib Gems is a family gem house in Colombo. For years the buying, cutting and selling were tracked on paper and in scattered spreadsheets, and stones were easy to lose track of.

Today everything runs on one connected system. The moment a rough stone is bought it gets a lot code, and that same record follows it through cutting, the vault, your reservation and the final invoice. That is how we can promise every gem here is real, certified and truly ours to sell.
